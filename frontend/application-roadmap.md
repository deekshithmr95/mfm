# MFM: Application Roadmap & Strategic Analysis

This document outlines the strategic vision for the **Mysore Farmer Marketplace (MFM)**, integrating market research with a digital implementation roadmap to solve both production-side and consumer-side agricultural challenges.

---

## 🌾 Part 1: Farmer Strategy (Production & Sustainability)
Focuses on empowering the producer and ensuring long-term farm viability.

| # | Problem | Solution | Digital Solvability |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :---: |
| **1** | **Market Volatility** | Direct Marketplace (e-NAM integration) | **⭐ High** |
| **2** | **Monsoon/Climate** | Hyper-local Weather/Crop Alerts | **🟠 Medium** |
| **3** | **Wildlife Conflict** | Geo-tagged Hazard Alerts (Elephant/Pig) | **🟠 Medium** |
| **4** | **Land Fragmentation** | FPO Management & Bulk Selling | **⭐ High** |
| **5** | **Knowledge Gap** | AI Agri-Consultants (AgriGPT) | **⭐ High** |

## 🛠️ Combined Implementation Roadmap

### **Phase 1: Traceability & Core E-comm (Current Focus)**
*   [ ] **Verified Farmer Badge:** Displaying farmer credentials and locations.
*   [ ] **Catch Weight Logic:** Pre-auth and partial refund for weight variance.
*   [ ] **Farmer Stories:** Enabling image/video uploads for farm provenance.

### **Phase 2: Collective Power & Community Safety**
*   [ ] **FPO Portal:** Tools for agricultural groups to manage bulk inventory.
*   [ ] **Hazard Alert System:** Push notifications for wildlife reports.
*   [ ] **Apartment Group Buying:** Zero-fee delivery for clustered orders.

### **Phase 3: Intelligence & Global Health**
*   [ ] **MFM-AgriGPT:** AI chatbot for crop advice & voice commerce.
*   [ ] **Subscription Boxes:** Automatic weekly CSA deliveries.
*   [ ] **Compost Cycle:** Integrating the kitchen-waste-to-farm feedback loop.
